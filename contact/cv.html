Brief CV:
  • [August, 2015] I graduated with a masters degree in Physics from Rheinische Friedrich-Wilhelms-Universität Bonn. I studied thermodynamic aspects of gravity from emergent gravity perspective during my master's thesis.
  • [September, 2015-November, 2016] I worked at Center of Advanced European Studies And Research in the department of Behaviour and Brain Organization. My primary work at CAESAR consists of extending the 3D model for pupil-tracking developed previously at lab [Wallace et al., Nature, 2013], to more generalized pupil shapes and implementation of the pupil-tracking algorithm in the eye-tracking software.
